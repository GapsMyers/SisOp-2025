## 2. Konversi bilangan desimal ke biner
**1234 (10)**  
Langkah-langkah:  
1234 √∑ 2 = 617 (Sisa 0)  
617 √∑ 2 = 308 (Sisa 1)  
308 √∑ 2 = 154 (Sisa 0)  
154 √∑ 2 = 77 (Sisa 0)  
77 √∑ 2 = 38 (Sisa 1)  
38 √∑ 2 = 19 (Sisa 0)  
19 √∑ 2 = 9 (Sisa 1)  
9 √∑ 2 = 4 (Sisa 1)  
4 √∑ 2 = 2 (Sisa 0)  
2 √∑ 2 = 1 (Sisa 0)  
1 √∑ 2 = 0 (Sisa 1)  
**Hasil:** 1234 (10) = 10011010010 (2)

## 6. Konversi bilangan desimal ke heksadesimal
